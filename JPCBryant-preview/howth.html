A Detective's Tale:

Howling Hills of Howth

Beautiful, Peaceful, Inspiring, and Heavenly Howth nestled on the hilly peninsula northeast of Dublin, Ireland. Visitors, denizens, and tourists trek the Cliffs for breathtaking views of the Irish Sea and the green hills of Howth where the misty fog, howling winds, and seaside lifestyles have taken root for thousands of years.

On this day though something terrible and dark has taken hold of the beautiful community. A death has occurred and whether the tragedy is an accident or murder seems to be up for debate. Young detective David Willits is called in to investigate the stranger aspects of the case. What he finds is shocking even to him! Will he solve the case and prevent more deaths!?
