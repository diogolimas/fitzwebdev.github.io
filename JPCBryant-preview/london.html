A Detective's Tale:

Latent Lines of London

The second book in the series, A Detective’s Tale. The story unfolds following the events that took place in Howth. Following up on the young detective’s discoveries. The secret organization of Sanctus Presidio launches an investigation into the mysterious organization that has been revealed.

What sinister plot are they devising in England? Is there a connection to their activities in Howth? Who are they exactly, and what are their goals? Follow along as these inquiries are researched and disturbing truths are brought to light.
