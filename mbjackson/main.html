Marianne B. Jackson is a full-time freelance artist and Illustrator with a bachelor’s degree in Studio Art and a master’s degree in art education who has been creating since she was a child. Besides being an artist, Marianne is a mother of three amazing kiddos, who when not looking, enjoy trying to embellish her artwork. Marianne is well rounded in a variety of art mediums and works for the buyer/author to help them receive the best quality and design they’ve dreamed of and deserve. Please see Marianne’s personal art portfolio for style and quality reference.
