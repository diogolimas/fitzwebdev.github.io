Our Mission

Our Mission is to create whole, respectful, honest, and responsible youth with opportunities to accomplish their dreams, creating a worth for future for the Youth of Garabito.

Herradura Garabito is not about winning matches or training elite athletes, it about finding a common passion that is universal that bring people together regardless of race, religion, financial status, or education and can change lives for the better.

Objectives

For kids in developing countries, soccer is a tool, with education as the motivation to retain them. Without education being a cornerstone, retention is impossible. Herradura Garabito instills good communication, with honest and transparent relationships between parents and children of our community based on the values of respect and honesty that we impress upon the youth of our cantón.

Sports Objectives

Soccer is the vehicle we use to involve kids from all backgrounds. To help allow and promote our youth to compete in tournaments on the national, Central American and International levels. Creating well-rounded and professional athletes to represent our cantón and our country in the best possible manner. Facilitate the best implementation and education of the sport.
